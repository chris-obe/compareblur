# Lens catalog attribution

`lensdb.lenses.json` is a trimmed copy of the **LensDB** lens database:

- Source: https://github.com/Luminoid/lens-db (lens.luminoid.dev)
- License: **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** (Attribution · NonCommercial · ShareAlike)

We use only a subset of fields (`id, brand, model, series, mounts, format,
lensType, focalMin, focalMax, apertureMaxWide, apertureMin, autofocus, priceUSD,
priceMSRPUSD`). Per the license:

- **Attribution** — credit LensDB (above) and link back.
- **NonCommercial** — this app must remain non-commercial.
- **ShareAlike** — this derived dataset is shared under the same CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Camera bodies and the curated Fujifilm GF lenses in `gear.seed.ts` are our own
data and are not part of the LensDB dataset.
